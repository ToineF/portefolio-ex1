Description :

This is the story of a tiny shroom who has to venture outside of his habitat after it got ravaged by a fire. It will have to fend for himself and find a new home, a new paradise for mushroom. To do this it'll have to take the control of bodies around it.

Credits :

Game Designer : Romain Horrenberger, Léa-Maria Ambrosio, Erwan Sevestre, Rafael Bauchiere.

Game Artist : Adam Lenglen, Mahiedine Boudrissa, Naomie Halter.

Game Programmer : Antoine Foucault, Charles Piercourt

Musics : Louis Gaubil


Submitted for the Global Game Jam 2023 whose theme was Roots.
